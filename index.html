finding comfort in
unsolvable tasks

Sometimes, it’s the tasks without solutions that set us free. In graphic design, where the pressure for the “right” decision can be overwhelming, embracing unsolvable tasks helps release the fear of mistakes. This project explores the beauty of starting without the fear of being wrong.
It invites designers to let go of perfection and enjoy the creative space where there are no mistakes—only possibilities.

Let yourself be guided by the freedom of creation.

a design project by Maike Müller
